Regulatory Compliance Framework

Key Regulations Affecting K-12 Education

FERPA (Family Educational Rights and Privacy Act)

  • Protects student education records
  • Requires parental consent for disclosure
  • Mandates secure handling of student information

COPPA (Children's Online Privacy Protection Act)

  • Protects children under 13 online
  • Requires verifiable parental consent
  • Limits data collection from minors

State Student Privacy Laws

  • California Student Privacy Acts
  • New York Student Data Privacy laws
  • Texas Student Privacy Protection laws

Best Practices for K-12 Privacy Compliance

Technology Implementation

  • Automated privacy compliance monitoring
  • Student data encryption at rest and in transit
  • Access control and audit logging systems
  • Regular privacy impact assessments

Policy and Training

  • Comprehensive student privacy policies
  • Regular staff training on privacy requirements
  • Parent notification and consent processes
  • Incident response procedures for privacy breaches
